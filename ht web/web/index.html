Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer, one of the most decorated and famous players in the world. He was born on February 5, 1985 in Madeira, Portugal. He played for clubs such as Sporting, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus and Al-Nassr (Saudi Arabia). Ronaldo is the winner of five Golden Balls, the titles of the best footballer in the world. He won the UEFA Champions League five times, became the champion of England, Spain and Italy. His style of play is known for its speed, accuracy of strikes, physical strength and leadership qualities. Ronaldo is also the top scorer in the history of the Champions League and the Portugal national team.
